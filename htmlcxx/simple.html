Visitors are always welcome, but please call first to arrange an appointment - anybody turning up "on spec" is invariably turned away, however far they have come. We don't like doing this (we're nice people really) but the workshop is often very busy, when we simply don't have the time to entertain visitors. Give a call first, book a time that is convenient for both of us and I will make sure there is time to talk, have a cup of tea and show you what you've come to see.
